Our Heritage

A Mission Woven
Across Continents

Founded by Brenda Williams in Freetown, Sierra Leone in 1994, NewLife Project grew from an evangelical outreach into an international force — weaving lives across Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, and now Canada.

In 2014, we established our Ottawa centre — a place where immigrant women and youth can build skills, find belonging, and create new futures.

"Empowering women and youth to achieve divine accomplishment and fulfil their purpose."
