About NewLife Project

A Mission Born in Sierra Leone,
Growing Strong in Ottawa

NewLife Project Inc. is a faith-rooted non-profit founded by Brenda Williams in Freetown, Sierra Leone in 1994. What began as an evangelical outreach campaign grew into an international organisation serving thousands across West Africa and Canada.

In 2014, we established our Ottawa centre — a place where immigrant women and youth find language support, vocational skills, mentorship, and an unbreakable community that helps them build independent, purposeful lives.

📋

Our Mission

"Empowering women and youth to achieve divine accomplishment and fulfil their purpose — rebuilding broken lives and facilitating total restoration."

Programs & Services

How We Serve Our Community

🌟

Youth Empowerment Program

Our Youth Empowerment Program is dedicated to nurturing at-risk youth aged 15–21. Through transformative mentorship, we inspire, guide, and equip young people with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to thrive personally and professionally.

Learn More →
🧵

Sewing & African Textiles

Intergenerational sewing classes teach African print fabric design — building marketable skills while celebrating cultural heritage. Participants can use these skills to launch their own businesses.

Learn More →
📖

French Tutoring Classes

Bilingual literacy classes for children and adults in Ottawa/Gatineau. English and French proficiency are essential for employment and integration in the National Capital Region.

Learn More →
👩‍👩‍👧

Women's World Forum

A monthly empowerment forum where women gather — online and in-person — to educate, inspire, and support each other. Topics span health, business, personal growth, and community.

Learn More →
💼

Entrepreneurship Program

Comprehensive support for women and youth to set up home-based businesses. We provide business skills training, product development support, and access to our online and in-store boutique.

Learn More →
🌍

Africa Relief Program

Sending essential supplies, equipment, and resources to partner communities in Sierra Leone, Côte d'Ivoire, and Ghana. Supporting sustainable development across three West African nations.

Learn More →
🎓

Internship Program

Providing meaningful work experience and career pathways for students and young professionals in the Ottawa/Gatineau area. Building resumes and professional networks for the future.

Learn More →
🛍️

NewLife Designs Shop

African print clothing and accessories celebrating Black culture — available online and in our Ottawa boutique. All proceeds support our community programs and outreach activities.
