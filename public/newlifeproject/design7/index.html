Our Roots

A Mission That
Grows Across
Continents

Founded by Brenda Williams in Freetown, Sierra Leone in 1994, NewLife Project grew from a local evangelical outreach into an international force for change — touching lives in Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, and Canada.

In 2014, we planted roots in Ottawa — growing programs that nurture immigrant women and youth through language, livelihoods, and unbreakable community bonds.

"Empowering women and youth to achieve divine accomplishment and fulfil their purpose."
