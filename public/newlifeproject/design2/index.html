Our Story

A Mission Born in
Freetown, 1994

NewLife Project was founded through an evangelical outreach campaign in Sierra Leone. Over three decades, it has grown from a local initiative to an international organisation touching lives in Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, and Canada.

In 2014, founder Brenda Williams established the Canadian centre in Ottawa to serve immigrant women and youth — offering programs that build language, livelihood, and life skills.
