Our Story

A Mission Born
in West Africa

NewLife Project Inc. was founded by Brenda Williams in Freetown, Sierra Leone through an evangelical outreach campaign in 1994. Over three decades, the mission expanded to Côte d'Ivoire and Ghana before establishing its Canadian home in Ottawa in 2014.

Today, we empower immigrant women and at-risk youth in Ottawa/Gatineau — building lives of economic independence, cultural pride, and restored purpose.

"Empowering women and youth to achieve divine accomplishment and fulfil their purpose."
