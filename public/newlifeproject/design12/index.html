Our Story

A Mission Born
in Freetown,
Sierra Leone

Founded in 1994 by Brenda Williams, NewLife Project began as an evangelical outreach in Sierra Leone. Over three decades, it grew to serve communities in Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, and Canada.

In 2014, we established our Ottawa centre — serving immigrant women and youth with free programs that build economic independence, language skills, and unbreakable community.

"Empowering women and youth to achieve divine accomplishment and fulfil their purpose."
