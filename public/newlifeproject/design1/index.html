Our Story

From Sierra Leone
to Ottawa — A Mission
That Crosses Borders

NewLife Project is a faith-rooted non-profit founded by Brenda Williams in Freetown, Sierra Leone. Born from an evangelical campaign in 1994, the project grew to touch lives in Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, and Sierra Leone — before establishing its Canadian home in Ottawa in 2014.

Today, we serve immigrant women, at-risk youth, and families in Ottawa/Gatineau through vocational training, mentorship, language support, and community-building programs that build economic independence and restore dignity.
