Our Story

Born in Sierra Leone.
Serving Ottawa.

NewLife Project was founded by Brenda Williams in Freetown, Sierra Leone through an evangelical outreach campaign in 1994. The mission grew from a single community to touch thousands of lives across Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, and Canada.

In 2014, Brenda established our Ottawa centre to serve immigrant women and youth — offering programs that build language skills, livelihoods, and unbreakable community ties.

"Empowering women and youth to achieve divine accomplishment and fulfil their purpose."
