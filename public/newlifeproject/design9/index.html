FEATURE

Empowering Immigrant Women & Youth Across Continents

NewLife Project Community

Founded in 1994 by Brenda Williams in Freetown, Sierra Leone, NewLife Project Inc. has grown from a local evangelical outreach into an international force for change — touching over 1,000 lives across West Africa and Canada.

Now in its 10th year of Canadian operations, the Ottawa-based non-profit continues to serve immigrant women and at-risk youth through eight unique programs offering vocational training, language support, entrepreneurship, and community.

"Empowering women and youth to achieve divine accomplishment and fulfil their purpose."

— Brenda Williams, Founder & CEO
