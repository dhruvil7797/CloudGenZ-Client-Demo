Our Story

✦
1994

The Beginning

Founded by Brenda Williams in Freetown, Sierra Leone through an evangelical outreach campaign — a single act of faith that grew into an international ministry.

2004

West Africa Expansion

The mission grew to serve communities in Côte d'Ivoire and Ghana through vocational training, health clinics, and relief programs reaching thousands.

2014

Ottawa, Canada

A Canadian centre was established in Ottawa — a new home for immigrant women and youth seeking community, skills, and purpose in their new country.

2024

10-Year Anniversary

A decade of Canadian service. Over 1,000 lives touched across four countries. Eight active programs. A community stronger than ever.
