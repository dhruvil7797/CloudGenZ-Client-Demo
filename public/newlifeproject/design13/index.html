Our Story

A Mission Born in
Freetown, Sierra Leone

NewLife Project Inc. was founded by Brenda Williams in Sierra Leone in 1994 through an evangelical outreach campaign. Over 30 years, the mission grew to touch lives across Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, and — since 2014 — Ottawa, Canada.

We serve immigrant women and at-risk youth through free programs that build language skills, vocational expertise, entrepreneurship, and unbreakable community.

"Empowering women and youth to achieve divine accomplishment and fulfil their purpose."
