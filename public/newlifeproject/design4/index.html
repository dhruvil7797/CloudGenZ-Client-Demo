Our Story

From Freetown
to Ottawa —
A 30-Year Mission

Founded in 1994 by Brenda Williams in Sierra Leone, NewLife Project has grown from a local evangelical outreach into an international organisation serving communities in West Africa and Canada.

In 2014, we opened our Ottawa centre — a home for immigrant women and youth to build skills, find community, and discover their purpose.
