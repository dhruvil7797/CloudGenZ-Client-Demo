A Mission Born
in West Africa

NewLife Project was founded through an evangelical campaign in Sierra Leone in 1994. Over 30 years, it grew to serve communities in Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, and — since 2014 — Ottawa, Canada.

Today, we serve immigrant women, at-risk youth, and families in Ottawa/Gatineau through free programs that build language, livelihoods, and connections.
