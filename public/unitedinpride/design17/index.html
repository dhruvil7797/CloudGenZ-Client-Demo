Our Community Space

Who We Are

A Place Where Everyone Belongs

United in Pride is a vibrant community centre dedicated to serving the 2SLGBTQIA+ community in Ottawa. We provide a safe, inclusive, and affirming space where individuals can connect, grow, and thrive.

Located in Ottawa on the unceded, unsurrendered territory of the Algonquin Anishinaabe people, we honour the land and its original stewards as we build a community rooted in respect, love, and solidarity.

Our Programs

Empowering our community through diverse programs and initiatives

🎨

Art & Creativity

Express yourself through painting, sculpture, digital art, and creative writing workshops led by queer artists from our community.

Support Groups

Facilitated peer support circles for various experiences — coming out, transitioning, family relationships, and mental health.

💬
📖

Education

Workshops on 2SLGBTQIA+ history, rights, health literacy, and allyship training for individuals and organizations.

Social Events

Game nights, movie screenings, potlucks, and seasonal celebrations that foster genuine connections and lasting friendships.

🎭
🧘

Wellness

Yoga, meditation, fitness classes, and holistic health programs designed for the unique needs of our community members.

Advocacy

Grassroots campaigns, policy education, and community organizing to advance 2SLGBTQIA+ rights and protections in Ottawa and beyond.
