Who We Are

Ottawa, ON — Unceded Algonquin Anishinaabe Territory

United in Pride is Ottawa's vibrant 2SLGBTQIA+ community centre, located on the unceded, unsurrendered territory of the Algonquin Anishinaabe people. Since 2018, we have been a beacon of hope, connection, and celebration for queer and trans communities across the National Capital Region.

Our centre provides a safe, affirming space where individuals of all identities can find support, build community, and thrive. From youth programs to elder circles, art workshops to advocacy campaigns, we serve the full spectrum of our diverse community.

We believe in the power of community to transform lives. Every program we offer, every event we host, and every conversation we facilitate is rooted in principles of equity, inclusion, and radical joy. Together, we are building a future where every 2SLGBTQIA+ person in Ottawa feels seen, valued, and celebrated.
