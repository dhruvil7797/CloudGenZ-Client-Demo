WHO WE ARE

United in Pride is a vibrant 2SLGBTQIA+ community centre located on the unceded, unsurrendered territory of the Algonquin Anishinaabe people in Odawa/Ottawa. We provide a bold, inclusive space where queer and trans communities come together to celebrate, support, and empower one another.

  • ◆ Inclusive programming for all ages
  • ◆ Mental health & peer support
  • ◆ Cultural events & art exhibitions
  • ◆ Advocacy & community organizing
