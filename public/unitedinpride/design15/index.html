Who We Are

United in Pride is a vibrant 2SLGBTQIA+ community centre located on the unceded, unsurrendered territory of the Algonquin Anishinaabe Nation in Ottawa (Odawa). We provide a safe, affirming space where Two-Spirit, lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, intersex, asexual, and all gender-diverse people can connect, heal, and thrive.
