About Us

Who We Are

United in Pride is a vibrant community centre dedicated to serving the 2SLGBTQIA+ community on the unceded, unsurrendered territory of the Algonquin Anishinaabe Nation in Odawa/Ottawa. We provide a safe, inclusive space where everyone can find support, connection, and belonging.
