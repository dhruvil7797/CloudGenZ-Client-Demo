Who We Are

United in Pride is a community centre for 2SLGBTQIA+ and other oppressed sexual and gender identities. Located in Ottawa on unceded Algonquin territory, we provide a welcoming space where everyone can find support, connection, and celebration.

Since our founding, we've grown from a small gathering into a vibrant hub serving thousands of community members through programmes, events, and advocacy work that centres the most marginalized voices.

Our Programmes

🎨

Art & Creativity

Express yourself through painting, sculpture, writing workshops, and collaborative art projects in a judgement-free studio.

💬

Support Groups

Peer-led circles for sharing experiences, processing challenges, and building resilience together in a confidential setting.

📚

Education

Workshops on queer history, allyship training, and skill-building sessions designed to empower and inform our community.

🎉

Social Events

Game nights, potlucks, movie screenings, and seasonal celebrations that bring our community together in joy.

🧘

Wellness

Yoga, meditation, counselling referrals, and holistic health programming centred on 2SLGBTQIA+ wellbeing.

✊

Advocacy

Policy engagement, community organizing, and rights-based campaigns that amplify queer and trans voices in Ottawa.
