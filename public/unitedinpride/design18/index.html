Who We Are

About Our Centre

Empowering Our Community Since 2000

United in Pride has been a cornerstone of Ottawa's 2SLGBTQIA+ community for over 25 years. We provide a welcoming, affirming space where individuals of all gender identities, sexual orientations, and expressions can find support, connection, and celebration.

Our centre offers comprehensive programs ranging from mental health support and legal aid to cultural events and youth leadership development. We believe in the power of community to create lasting change.

Located in the heart of Ottawa, we serve over 15,000 community members annually through more than 200 events and 50+ dedicated support programs.

What We Offer

Our Programs

Youth Programs

Safe spaces, mentorship, and leadership development for 2SLGBTQIA+ youth ages 13–24. Weekly meetups, skill workshops, and peer support groups.

Learn More

Mental Health Support

Free confidential counselling, support groups, and crisis intervention services with 2SLGBTQIA+-affirming therapists and counsellors.

Learn More

Legal Aid Clinic

Pro bono legal assistance for name changes, discrimination cases, immigration support, and human rights complaints.

Learn More

Senior Connect

Social programs, wellness activities, and peer support designed specifically for 2SLGBTQIA+ seniors and elders in our community.

Learn More

Cultural Events

Art exhibitions, film screenings, drag shows, open mic nights, and cultural celebrations that honour the diversity of our community.

Learn More

Housing Support

Emergency shelter referrals, transitional housing assistance, and affordable housing navigation for community members in need.
