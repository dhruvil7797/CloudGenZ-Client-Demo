Who We Are

United in Pride is a community centre dedicated to serving 2SLGBTQIA+ individuals and other oppressed sexual and gender identities in Ottawa. We provide a safe, affirming space where everyone can find belonging, support, and celebration.

Located on the unceded, unsurrendered territory of the Algonquin Anishinaabe people, we honour the land and its original stewards as we build a more inclusive future together.

Our programs span art, wellness, education, advocacy, and social connection — all designed to uplift and empower our vibrant community.
