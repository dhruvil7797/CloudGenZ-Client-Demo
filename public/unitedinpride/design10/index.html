WHO WE ARE

Community for 2SLGBTQIA+ Identities

United in Pride is a community centre for 2SLGBTQIA+ and other oppressed sexual and gender identities and expressions.

Located in Ottawa on unceded Algonquin territory, we offer resources, events, and social programming. Our work celebrates, supports, and prioritizes marginalized sexual orientations, gender identities, and expressions.
