Our Services

How We Support the World's
Seafarers in Canada

As part of a 160+ year legacy, we offer practical, emotional, and spiritual support at Canadian ports, ensuring no seafarer is alone.

Practical Support

SIM cards, Wi-Fi, transportation, healthcare and legal assistance

Mental & Emotional Health

Crisis support, professional care and a listening ear

Spiritual Care

Open to all faiths — prayers, blessings, and compassionate presence

Advocacy & Rights

Protecting seafarer rights with onboard visits and legal guidance

Community Connection

Centres, shared meals and local partners creating belonging
