Our Services

How We Support the World's Seafarers in Canada

As part of a 160+ year legacy, we offer practical, emotional, and spiritual support at Canadian ports, ensuring no seafarer is alone.

FIND RESOURCES
01

Practical Support

SIM cards, Wi-Fi, transportation, healthcare and legal assistance to keep seafarers connected and cared for.

02

Mental & Emotional Health

Crisis support, professional care, and a listening ear when it's needed most at sea.

03

Spiritual Care

Prayers, blessings, and compassionate presence open to all faiths and backgrounds.

04

Advocacy & Rights

Protecting seafarer rights through onboard visits, hospital and legal guidance.

05

Community Connection

Centres, shared meals, and local partners creating moments of belonging at port.
